The Why

Before you start FMOD scripting like there's no tomorrow you should set this to speed up your development cycle. This is because you need to reload to make your script changes take effect.

The How

Press Ctrl+Shift+P to open the Preferences window and press the Shortcuts tab. Add a shortcut to Action Scripts: Reload, I recommend Ctrl+R that is available by default.
