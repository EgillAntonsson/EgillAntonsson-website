Jón Páll Sigmarsson who was the first man in history to win the World Strongest Man four times. He is a big inspiration to me as he was the first Icelandic athlete to be the best in the world in a sport, along being charismatic and entertaining. My parents knew Jón's dad and they took a photo of Jón and me when I was around 6 years old.

In 1987 while Deadlifting 455 kg (his final lift was 523 kg), Jón shouted "There is no reason to be alive if you can't do Deadlift!", and as fate would have it he died in 1993, 32 years of age, while Deadlifting in his gym, but his spirit lives on in the hearts of many.
